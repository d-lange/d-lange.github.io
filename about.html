About

Hi, my name is David Lange and I'm currently a full stack web development student at Dev Bootcamp in New York City. Prior to this, I spent the last two and a half years working in Executive Search as a Recruiter placing executives in marketing, advertising, market research, analytics, and luxury positions.

I love building things and understanding why and how things work. When I'm not programming, some of my other passions include music as I've been in a few bands in the past and still compose music for film projects. I'm also an avid tennis player. I played in college for Roger Williams University bringing the team to the NCAA tournament for the first time in school history!

Feel free to keep track of my projects on this site as well as my blog, which highlights and documents what I'm learning throughout my Dev Bootcamp experience.
