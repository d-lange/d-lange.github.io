ABOUT ME

Welcome to my site! My name is David Lange and I'm currently a web development student at Dev Bootcamp in New York City. I have spent the last two and a half years working in Executive Search as a Recruiter placing executives in marketing, advertising, market research, analytics, and luxury positions. I am now in the process of learning full stack development. I'm truly excited to learn computer programming to gain technical skills that will forever benefit my career and overall creativeness and give me the power to create awesome and useful tools.


Some of my other passions include music as I've been in a few bands in the past and still compose music for film projects. I'm also an avid tennis player. I played in college for Roger Williams University bringing the team to the NCAA tournament for the first time in school history.


Feel free to keep track of my upcoming projects on this site as well as my blog, which highlights and documents what I'm learning throughout my Dev Bootcamp experience.
